About me

Julia is a multidisciplinary creative and entrepreneur working at the intersection of visual design, branding, and emotional storytelling. She creates cohesive visual worlds through illustration, surface pattern design, and thoughtfully built brand identities, often blending softness, nostalgia, and depth with strategic clarity.

With a strong understanding of both aesthetics and business, Julia develops digital products, brand systems, and content designed to resonate emotionally while remaining commercially viable. Her work is informed by curiosity, self-directed learning, and a highly intuitive yet analytic approach to problem solving.

Julia is currently expanding her practice across digital design, product development, and web-development, while building multiple creative brands and platforms
